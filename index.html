Welcome to Care Plus Health Services

Care Plus is a group home, meaning a home like residential facility in a given community where individual with physical or mental disability reside.

We believe that allowing mentally challenged people to live in the private homes, favorizes their integration in the community and most of all, increases public awareness on the crucial problem of mental illness.

Through the opening of Care Plus, we like to bring our modest contribution to the resolution of the mental health stigma.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a healthy, therapeutic, loving and safe environment where our residents interact in harmony with the support of our staff.

Our values:

We value the people we serve by giving them respect and dignity.

  • Knowledge is power; understand mental health

  • Quality of services to our residents

  • Commitment: we are committed to the wellbeing of our residents

  • We value integrity in health care

  • Collaboration amount care providers.

Our Mission

We care for the well-being of our residents. Our goal is to improve the quality of life of mentally challenged people in a home like setting.

By providing our residents the best compassionate and competent care for their needs, we give them the power to be the best they can be and help them reach their maximum individual potential. To facilitate their integration in the community.

Our Services

By the creation of a home safe like environment, we want to maximize the level of independence of our residents and improve their daily living skills.

A thorough health assessment and evaluation of each individual is done upon admission allowing an efficient implementation of a care plan specific to each client.

Self-care skills are taught and encouraged as possible (Bathing, dressing, meal preparation, self-feeding, home maintenance).

  • We encourage social interaction and promote healthy relationship

  • We provide assistance with medications administration and management

  • We encourage sports activities as possible

  • We provide 24 hours, residential supervision

  • We provide transportation for our residents to: medical appointments/outing

  • We educate our staff and participate in abuse and neglect prevention.

The Role of Personal Care Assistant at Care Plus:

The Personal Care Assistant at Care Plus are the back bone of the company because they carry out the implementation of the care plan and interact closely and daily with the residents.

Care Plus support an atmosphere of teamwork, learning, growth and collaboration amount the care providers.
